About Business Brain

Business Brain is a software product developed and operated by BLACKLINE SOLUTIONS S.R.L., a company registered in Romania.

Business Brain is currently under development. Its purpose is to help founders build a grounded understanding of their business using connected business evidence — the sources a company already produces — while keeping a clear distinction between what has been observed and what has been inferred.

The company builds Business Brain as a professional instrument for founders. The product is not yet publicly available, and this website describes what it is intended to do rather than a released commercial offering.
