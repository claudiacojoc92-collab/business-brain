Grounded business intelligence for founders.

Business Brain is being developed as a professional instrument that helps founders understand their business through the evidence their company already produces.

What Business Brain is for

Founders make decisions from an incomplete picture. Business Brain is designed to build a grounded understanding of a business from the sources it already produces — always separating what has been observed from what has been inferred, so a founder can see where their stated direction and their real evidence pull apart.

It connects a business's own sources only with the founder's explicit authorization, and it is built to be careful about the difference between evidence and assumption. The product is not yet publicly available.
