Privacy Policy

Business Brain · Last updated: July 6, 2026

Business Brain (“we”, “us”, “our”) helps founders understand how their audience responds to their business. This Privacy Policy explains what Meta data — from Facebook and Instagram — we access when you connect your Meta account, why we access it, how we protect it, and how you can delete it.

What Meta data we access

When you choose to connect your Meta account, and only with your explicit consent, we access the following on a read-only basis:

  • The list of Facebook Pages you manage (pages_show_list) — so you can select which Page to connect.
  • Your selected Page’s audience data, such as fan and follower counts (pages_read_engagement) — used as a measure of your audience.
  • The basic profile of the Instagram Business/Creator account linked to your Page — username, follower count, and media count (instagram_basic).

We do not post, publish, comment, send messages, or modify anything on your Facebook or Instagram accounts. Our access is strictly read-only.

Why we access it

We use this data solely to show you an analysis of your own business — how your audience and online presence compare with your stated positioning and goals. It is presented only to you, the connected account holder.

How we store and protect it

Access credentials and any stored data are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. Credentials are never displayed to you or to any third party. We do not sell, rent, or share your Meta data with anyone.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your connection only while you use the service. You can delete your data at any time by disconnecting your Meta account in the app, which immediately deletes the stored credential and revokes our access at Meta, or by emailing us (see below). Full instructions are on our Data Deletion page.
