Data Deletion Instructions

Business Brain · Last updated: July 6, 2026

You can delete all data that Business Brain holds about your Meta (Facebook and Instagram) account at any time, using either method below.

How to delete your data

  1. Disconnect in the app. Open Business Brain and disconnect your Meta account. This immediately deletes the stored access credential and revokes Business Brain’s access to your Facebook and Instagram data at Meta.
  2. Email us. Send a message to privacy@getbusinessbrain.com with the subject “Delete my data”. We will delete all associated data within 30 days and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

We store only an encrypted access credential and the read-only Facebook Page and Instagram profile data described in our Privacy Policy (your list of Pages, a Page’s fan/follower counts, and the linked Instagram account’s username, follower count, and media count). Deleting removes all of it. We do not sell or share this data with anyone.
